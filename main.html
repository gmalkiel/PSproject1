Israel In Green

all you need to know for nature

The flora of the Land of Israel is a general name for plants that grow wild throughout the land, apart from cultivated plants that grow due to human activity. The flora in Israel includes about 2,700 species divided into 130 families, of which 264 species have been defined as legally protected plants. The vegetation in Israel is characterized by great diversity due to the many different climate zones of the Land of Israel. Starting from the Iran-Tor ani region that characterizes the north of Israel and ending with the desert climate of the Negev.
